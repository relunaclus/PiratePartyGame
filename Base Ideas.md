# parrot mimic game
 - parrot theming - pirates
 - players are shown inputs
 - players must copy inputs
 - failing to copy inputs results in player being eliminated
 - simple game, should last 15-30s on average
 - time limit

    - list of possible inputs - possibly directional
    - round/wave based system
    - parrot holds sign showing inputs, + sound effect
    - players are given a short window of time to copy
    - copying incorrectly = eliminated
    - copying correctly = move to next wave

# cannon firing game
- cannon theme
- 1v1 minigame
- one player has a cannon and can shoot, albeit at a slow rate, at another player
- the other player has to try and dodge the cannonballs

    - aiming mechanics - directional movement
    - directional movement for dodging player
    - cannonball projectile - not a straight line
    - time limit so dodging player can win

# treasure hunt game
- 2 player cooperative minigame
- one player has an area to explore and the goal of finding a treasure chest
- the other player recieves directions in the form of pings, like a metal detector
- the player with directions has to guide the other player to find the treasure chest

    - directional movement for exploring player
    - directional pings for guiding player
    - time limit to allow loss

# boat game
- 2v2
- each team is on a boat
- 1 captain, 1 cannoner per boat
- captain has to steer boat to avoid obstacles and other danger
- cannoner has to aim and fire cannonballs at the other team to damage their boat
- a team loses when their boat is sunk

    - directional movement - steering
    - directional aiming of cannons
    - boat hp system
    - boat balance system
    - random obstacles - driftwood, sea monsters, sharks, seaweed, nets, ect.
    - time limit

# hook fishing game
- each player has a hook
- on ocean
- have to catch fish
- basically just the club penguin ice fishing game / fnaf world fishing game
- multiple fish with different  point values
- player with most points at end of time limit wins

    - left/right movement of hook
    - button to let hook down, to catch fish
    - different point value fish
    - point tracking
    - time limit
